About Me

An environmental management enthusiast advocating for climate action through adaptation and mitigation with specific focus on Climate Change Vulnerability and Adaptation. Currently working as a Scientific Officer in the Climate Change Department of the Bayelsa State Ministry of Environment , Nigeria.

Graduated from the Niger Delta University, Wilberforce Island, Bayelsa State, Nigeria, with a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) Degree in Geography and Environmental Management and Post Graduate Diploma (PGD) in Marine Surveying.

Interests

Thompson is interested in researching and proffering local climate change mitigation and adaptation solutions to vulnerable rural dwellers of Bayelsa State.

Sustainable environmental management practices in the Niger Delta region of Nigeria where clean water, clear/clean air is still a challenge.

Clean, sustainable and socioeconomically prosperous environment through ecotourism development in Nigeria's Niger Delta region (Bayelsa in particular)

The adoption of modern geospatial technologies in providing workable solutions to climate change and other environmental challenged faced in the Niger Delta region of Nigeria.
